Lemon-Herb Grilled Wings

Ingredients

  • Grill
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Chicken Wings
  • Lemons
  • Fresh Lemon Thyme or Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Procedure

  1. Cut lemons into quarters
  2. Place 3-4 chicken wings into each foil packet
  3. Liberally salt and pepper them
  4. Place fresh lemon thyme or thyme on top and around the wings
  5. Squeeze the lemon quarters over the open foil packets, and place the lemons on top
  6. Seal and double or triple wrap the foil packets, being sure to crimp at the top, but leave a small hole in the center for ventillation
  7. Place in a hot oven ~400Â°F or on a grill at similar higher temperature for at least a half hour.
  8. Once packets have cooked for a while and the liquid has boiled, carefully open the packets
  9. Transfer back to grill and turn up heat to brown/char the outside
  10. Serve. I recommend with some white rice and fresh vegetables.
