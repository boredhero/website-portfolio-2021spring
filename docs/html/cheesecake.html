Cheesecake

Ingredients

Cheesecake Crust

  • 9-inch springform pan
  • Large deep pan that springform pan can fit inside. Must be at least as tall as springform pan as well
  • 1 3/4 cups Crumbled graham crackers
  • 1/2 stick melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Cheesecake Filling

  • Mixer (preferred but you can do it manually if you don't care about sore hands/arms later)
  • 4 packages of cream cheese (8 oz each)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs

Recommended Toppings

  • Cherry pie filling
  • Chocolate (shaved with cheese grater)
  • Whipped cream

Procedure

Cheesecake Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Combine crumbled graham crackers, melted butter, and sugar
  3. Press into the bottom and sides of the springform pan
  4. Bake crust 8-10 minutes until golden. Set aside to cool.
  5. Wrap springform pan (except top) in foil
  6. Make filling

Cheesecake Filling

  1. Cream together the cheese and sugar
  2. Add vanilla and eggs (one at a time), continuing to cream ingredients together.
  3. Pour over crust
  4. Place into pan and fill that pan with water 3/4 way to the height of the springform pan
  5. Bake at 350°F for 60-70 minutes. Check it around 60 minutes.
  6. If the outer rim is set and the center is slightly jiggly, your cheesecake is done.
  7. Cool at room temperature for an hour
  8. Cover top with foil and chill in fridge for 6-12 hours.
  9. Carefully remove and serve. Add any toppings you like.
