Cooking Services and Lessons

What you will learn

  1. How to cook for yourself is a useful skill to help you save money, impress your friends, eat healthier food, and can be fun and relaxing too!
  2. Ingredient selection is the first step in making good food. You need to know what fresh, quality vegetables and meat look, feel, and smell like.
  3. Basic cooking techniques such as searing, sautéing, steaming, and more all operate on scientific principles. Understading these basic principles will help you use techniques to their fullest.
  4. Advanced techniques and tools such as Sous vide and food chemistry are things that the vast majority of people are clueless about, but you can use them to make restaurant-grade food in your own home.
  5. Fun and simple recipes for any budget or experience level that will knock your socks off. Some of these are free for you to view on this website.
